## Overview

Fetch and process a TikTok video in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Copy a TikTok video link">
    ````
    Example: https://www.tiktok.com/@user/video/123456
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send API request">
    ````bash
    curl -X POST https://api.fetchmedia.com/v1/fetch \
      -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
      -d '{"url": "PASTE_TIKTOK_URL_HERE"}'
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download your video">
    ````
    Find the video URL in the JSON response and download using your browser or curl.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
