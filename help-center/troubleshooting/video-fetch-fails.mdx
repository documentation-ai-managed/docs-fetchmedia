---
title: Video Fetch Fails
description: Resolve fetch failures from social platforms in FetchMedia.
---

## Symptoms

- API returns error: "Failed to fetch video"
- No video in response

## Causes

- Invalid or unsupported social link
- Account is out of quota

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the Link">
    ````
    Make sure the URL is correct and public.
    Example: https://www.tiktok.com/@user/video/1234
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Quota">
    ````
    Log in to FetchMedia dashboard and check your plan limits.
    Upgrade if your quota is exhausted.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
